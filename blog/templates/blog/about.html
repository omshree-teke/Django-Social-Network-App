About WordFlow ✨

WordFlow is a social platform built for writers, thinkers, and curious minds. Share your thoughts, connect with like-minded people, chat in real time, and stay updated with notifications.

Features

📝

Write & Share

Create posts and share your ideas with the world.

💬

Real-time Chat

Chat instantly with your friends using WebSockets.

🔔

Notifications

Stay updated with likes, comments and friend requests.

👥

Friend System

Send friend requests and build your network.

📹

Video Calls

Connect face to face with your friends.

🔍

Search

Find posts and people easily with search.

Tech Stack

🐍 Python  |  🌐 Django  |  ⚡ Django Channels  |  🔌 WebSockets  |  🗄️ SQLite  |  🎨 Bootstrap
